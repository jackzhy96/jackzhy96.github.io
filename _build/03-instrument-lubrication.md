## Overview

Development of a novel approach for surgical instrument lubrication and instrument internal cable tension recovery, extending the usable lifetime of expensive surgical instruments.

## Technical Details

- Developed lubrication protocols for instrument mechanisms
- Created cable tension recovery procedures
- Documented maintenance schedules and best practices
- Reduced instrument replacement costs significantly

## Impact

These maintenance approaches help research labs maintain their surgical instruments at lower cost while ensuring consistent performance for experiments.
