## Overview

Development of a novel approach for surgical instrument lubrication and instrument internal cable tension recovery, extending the usable lifetime of expensive surgical instruments.

## Technical Details

- Developed lubrication protocols for instrument mechanisms
- Created cable tension recovery procedures
- Improved the instrument gripper control performance

## Impact

These maintenance approaches can help research labs maintain their surgical instruments at lower cost while ensuring consistent performance for experiments.
