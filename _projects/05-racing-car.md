## Overview

Course project at UC Berkeley focused on designing dynamics models and control systems for autonomous racing cars.

## Technical Details

- Developed vehicle dynamics model for high-speed racing scenarios
- Designed control algorithms for path tracking and speed optimization
- Implemented and tested controllers in simulation environment
